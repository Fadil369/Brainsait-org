### Rejection Reason Codes (critical for ClaimLinc denial analytics)
**AD (Adjudication/Clinical):**
- AD-1-x: Diagnosis/procedure inconsistency with provider, specialty, encounter, age, gender
- AD-2-x: Date issues (DOB after service, death before service, prior to coverage, duplicate, time limit expired)
- AD-3-x: Authorization not required, bundled code, DRG calculation errors

**CV (Coverage):**
- CV-1-x: Out of network, not covered, pre-existing, annual limit exceeded, 14-day follow-up
- CV-2-1: Patient not a covered member
- CV-3-x: Frequency exceeded (period, life, policy, SAR threshold)
- CV-4-x: Drug/medication issues (dose, duration, formulary, FDA approval, device mismatch)

**MN (Medical Necessity):**
- MN-1-1: Not clinically justified per clinical practice guideline
- MN-1-2: Patient in hospice/palliative care

**SE (Supporting Evidence):**
- SE-1-x: Missing vital signs, HPI, chief complaint, physical exam, PMH, lab results, diagnosis type, treatment plan, quantity, patient occupation

**BE (Billing/Administrative):**
- BE-1-1: Co-pay not collected
- BE-1-2: Service not performed
- BE-1-3: Not compliant with contractual agreement
- BE-1-4: PA required but not obtained
- BE-1-5: Claim inconsistent with pre-authorized services
- BE-1-6: Calculation discrepancy
- BE-1-7: Incorrect billing regime
- BE-1-10: Fraud

**WI code (seen in Riyadh PA rejections)** — likely "Wrong Information" = AD-1-9 (Mismatch in member information)
