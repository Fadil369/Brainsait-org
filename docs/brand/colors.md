# Brand Colors

## Overview

BrainSAIT's color palette creates a professional, trustworthy, and innovative visual identity for healthcare AI.

---

## Primary Colors

### BrainSAIT Blue
- **Hex:** #0066CC
- **RGB:** 0, 102, 204
- **Usage:** Primary actions, links, key UI elements

### Deep Navy
- **Hex:** #1A365D
- **RGB:** 26, 54, 93
- **Usage:** Headers, primary text, emphasis

### Teal Accent
- **Hex:** #00A3A3
- **RGB:** 0, 163, 163
- **Usage:** Highlights, success states, accents

---

## Secondary Colors

### Light Blue
- **Hex:** #E6F3FF
- **RGB:** 230, 243, 255
- **Usage:** Backgrounds, cards

### Cool Gray
- **Hex:** #6B7280
- **RGB:** 107, 114, 128
- **Usage:** Secondary text, borders

### White
- **Hex:** #FFFFFF
- **Usage:** Backgrounds, cards

---

## Functional Colors

### Success Green
- **Hex:** #10B981
- **Usage:** Success states, positive indicators

### Warning Amber
- **Hex:** #F59E0B
- **Usage:** Warnings, caution states

### Error Red
- **Hex:** #EF4444
- **Usage:** Errors, destructive actions

### Info Blue
- **Hex:** #3B82F6
- **Usage:** Information, notifications

---

## Color Accessibility

- All text meets WCAG 2.1 AA standards
- Sufficient contrast ratios
- Color-blind friendly combinations

---

## Usage Guidelines

### Do
- Use primary blue for main CTAs
- Maintain consistent usage
- Follow accessibility guidelines

### Don't
- Use too many colors
- Override functional meanings
- Reduce contrast
