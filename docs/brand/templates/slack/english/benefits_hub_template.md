## Benefits FAQ

### Medical Insurance

**Q: Who is covered under my medical insurance?**
A: Coverage depends on your tier. Entry-level covers self, with family coverage increasing based on level.

**Q: How do I add a newborn to my insurance?**
A: Submit the birth certificate to HR within 30 days. Contact @hr-team for the form.

**Q: Which hospitals are in-network?**
A: [Link to provider directory]. Major hospitals in Riyadh, Jeddah, and Dammam included.

### Learning & Development

**Q: How do I use my learning budget?**
A: Submit a request in #learning-requests with course details. Manager approval required for amounts over 5K SAR.

**Q: What certifications are bonus-eligible?**
A: AWS, GCP, FHIR, HL7, CISA, CISSP, PMP, HIPAA/PDPL certifications qualify.

### Time Off

**Q: How do I request time off?**
A: Use the Time Off bot in #time-off-requests or submit through [HR Portal].

**Q: Can I carry over unused leave?**
A: Up to [X] days carry over to next year. Use or lose by Q1 end.
