## Platform Strategy

### LinkedIn (Primary)

```
📊 LinkedIn Focus
├── Audience: Healthcare executives, IT leaders, RCM professionals
├── Content: Thought leadership, case studies, product updates
├── Frequency: 3-5 posts per week
├── Tone: Professional, insightful, data-driven
└── Goals: Brand awareness, lead generation, talent attraction
```

### X (Twitter)

```
🐦 X Focus
├── Audience: Healthcare tech community, Saudi tech ecosystem
├── Content: News, quick insights, event coverage
├── Frequency: 5-7 posts per week
├── Tone: Engaging, timely, conversational
└── Goals: Awareness, community building
```

### YouTube (Future)

```
📹 YouTube Focus
├── Audience: Healthcare professionals, prospects
├── Content: Demos, webinars, customer stories
├── Frequency: 2-4 videos per month
├── Tone: Educational, professional
└── Goals: Product education, SEO
```

## Brand Voice Guidelines

### Do's

- Use data and specific metrics
- Reference NPHIES, PDPL, Saudi healthcare context
- Share customer success (with permission)
- Engage thoughtfully with comments
- Use bilingual content strategically (EN primary, AR for reach)

### Don'ts

- Make unsubstantiated claims
- Share customer info without approval
- Engage in competitive attacks
- Post controversial non-industry topics
- Use excessive emojis or informal tone
