## Week 1: Foundation

### Day 1: Welcome & Setup

- [ ] **IT Setup**: Laptop, Google Workspace, Slack, GitHub access
- [ ] **HR Essentials**: PDPL data handling training, benefits enrollment
- [ ] **Meet Your Buddy**: Paired with an onboarding buddy
- [ ] **First 1:1**: 30-min intro with your direct manager

### Day 2-3: Healthcare Industry Context

- [ ] **Saudi Healthcare Landscape**: Market overview, Vision 2030
- [ ] **NPHIES Deep Dive**: National claims exchange platform
- [ ] **Payer Ecosystem**: Bupa Arabia, Tawuniya, Medgulf, and major TPAs
- [ ] **RCM Fundamentals**: Revenue cycle management basics

### Day 4-5: BrainSAIT Platform

- [ ] **Linc Agent Overview**: ClaimLinc, PolicyLinc, DocsLinc, RadioLinc, Voice2Care
- [ ] **Product Demo**: End-to-end claims processing workflow
- [ ] **Customer Personas**: Hospital RCM teams, insurance companies, clinics
- [ ] **Value Proposition**: 50% rejection reduction, 98% clean claim rate
