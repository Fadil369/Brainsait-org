## Overview

Manage BrainSAIT partner ecosystem—EMR vendors, implementation partners, resellers, and technology partners—with structured enablement, deal registration, and co-selling workflows.

## Channel Structure

### Partner Channels

| Channel | Purpose | Members |
|---------|---------|---------|
| `#partner-[name]` | Dedicated partner collaboration | Partner team + BrainSAIT |
| `#partner-deals` | Deal registration and co-sell | Partner managers |
| `#partner-enablement` | Training and certification | All partners |
| `#partner-announcements` | Product updates, releases | All partners |

### Internal Channels

| Channel | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| `#partnerships-internal` | Partner strategy discussions |
| `#partner-support` | Partner technical issues |

## Partner Tiers

| Tier | Requirements | Benefits |
|------|--------------|----------|
| **Registered** | Agreement signed | 15-20% margin, basic training |
| **Silver** | 2+ implementations, 250K SAR annual | 20% margin, priority support |
| **Gold** | 5+ implementations, 1M SAR annual | 25% margin, co-marketing funds |
| **Platinum** | 10+ implementations, 3M SAR annual | 30% margin, executive sponsorship |

## Partner Onboarding

### Week 1: Foundation

- [ ] Partner agreement signed
- [ ] Partner portal access provisioned
- [ ] Partner manager assigned
- [ ] Kickoff meeting completed

### Week 2-3: Enablement

- [ ] **Sales Certification** (4 hours)
  - BrainSAIT value proposition
  - Agent overview (ClaimLinc, PolicyLinc, DocsLinc)
  - Competitive positioning
  - Qualification and discovery

- [ ] **Technical Certification** (16 hours)
  - Platform architecture
  - NPHIES integration
  - API and integration patterns
  - Troubleshooting basics

### Week 4: Go-Live

- [ ] First deal registered
- [ ] Co-selling process confirmed
- [ ] Support escalation path documented
- [ ] Marketing materials distributed

- **Location:** [City, Region]
- **Size:** [Beds / Monthly Claims]

### Opportunity Details

- **Products:** ClaimLinc / PolicyLinc / DocsLinc / etc.
- **Estimated Value:** [X] SAR
- **Expected Close:** [Date]
- **Decision Makers:** [Names, Titles]

### Partner Role

- [ ] Lead source (partner-sourced)
- [ ] Co-sell (BrainSAIT-sourced)

- [ ] Resale
