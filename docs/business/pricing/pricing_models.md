# Pricing Models

## Overview

BrainSAIT offers flexible pricing models designed to align value delivery with customer investment. Our pricing structure supports various organization sizes and use cases while ensuring positive ROI.

---

## Pricing Philosophy

### Core Principles

1. **Value-Based:** Pricing reflects measurable value delivered
2. **Transparent:** Clear, predictable costs
3. **Scalable:** Grows with customer success
4. **Flexible:** Options for different needs

---

## Pricing Models

### Model 1: Per-Claim Pricing

**Best For:** Enterprise and mid-market with variable volume

**Structure:**
- Fee per processed claim
- Volume-based tiers
- Monthly invoicing

**Pricing Tiers:**

| Monthly Volume | Price/Claim | Discount |
|----------------|-------------|----------|
| 1-5,000 | 5.00 SAR | Base |
| 5,001-15,000 | 4.00 SAR | 20% |
| 15,001-30,000 | 3.50 SAR | 30% |
| 30,001-50,000 | 3.00 SAR | 40% |
| 50,001+ | Custom | Negotiated |

**Inclusions:**
- Full platform access
- All agent capabilities
- Standard integrations
- Support (business hours)

**Add-ons:**
- Premium support: +15%
- Custom integrations: Project-based
- On-site training: 10K SAR/day

### Model 2: Subscription Pricing

**Best For:** SME with predictable volume

**Tiers:**

#### Starter
**500 claims/month**
- Price: 2,000 SAR/month
- Agents: ClaimLinc basic
- Support: Email
- Users: 3

#### Professional
**2,000 claims/month**
- Price: 6,000 SAR/month
- Agents: ClaimLinc + PolicyLinc
- Support: Email + Chat
- Users: 10

#### Business
**5,000 claims/month**
- Price: 12,000 SAR/month
- Agents: All healthcare agents
- Support: Priority
- Users: Unlimited

**Annual Discount:** 15% for annual prepay

### Model 3: Hybrid Pricing

**Best For:** Organizations with seasonal variation

**Structure:**
- Base subscription fee
- Per-claim fee above threshold
- Best of both models

**Example:**
- Base: 5,000 SAR/month (includes 1,000 claims)
- Overage: 3.50 SAR/claim above 1,000
