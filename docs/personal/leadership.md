# Leadership

## Overview

Leadership principles for building and leading high-performing teams in technology and healthcare.

---

## Leadership Philosophy

### Servant Leadership
- Serve the team's needs
- Remove obstacles
- Enable success
- Share credit

### Lead by Example
- Demonstrate values
- Show vulnerability
- Take responsibility
- Maintain standards

---

## Core Competencies

### Vision
- Define clear direction
- Inspire commitment
- Connect to purpose
- Paint the picture

### Communication
- Listen actively
- Speak clearly
- Write effectively
- Adapt to audience

### Decision Making
- Gather input
- Consider options
- Decide timely
- Commit fully

### People Development
- Hire well
- Coach continuously
- Delegate appropriately
- Give feedback

---

## Team Building

### Culture
- Define values
- Model behaviors
- Celebrate wins
- Learn from failures

### Psychological Safety
- Encourage questions
- Welcome disagreement
- Admit mistakes
- Support risk-taking

### High Performance
- Clear expectations
- Right resources
- Regular feedback
- Recognition

---

## Difficult Situations

### Conflict Resolution
1. Acknowledge the issue
2. Understand perspectives
3. Find common ground
4. Agree on solution

### Tough Conversations
- Be direct but kind
- Focus on behavior
- Listen fully
- Agree on next steps

---

## Continuous Growth

- Seek feedback
- Find mentors
- Read widely
- Reflect regularly
