# Ethics & AI

## Overview

Ethical considerations for developing and deploying AI systems in healthcare and beyond.

---

## Core Principles

### Beneficence
- Create positive impact
- Improve outcomes
- Reduce suffering
- Enable human flourishing

### Non-Maleficence
- Avoid harm
- Consider unintended consequences
- Fail safely
- Protect vulnerable

### Autonomy
- Respect human agency
- Maintain transparency
- Enable informed decisions
- Preserve choice

### Justice
- Fair distribution of benefits
- Equitable access
- Avoid bias
- Consider all stakeholders

---

## AI-Specific Considerations

### Transparency
- Explainable decisions
- Clear limitations
- Honest capabilities
- Open about AI use

### Bias & Fairness
- Diverse training data
- Regular audits
- Bias mitigation
- Fair outcomes

### Privacy
- Data minimization
- Consent
- Security
- De-identification

### Accountability
- Clear responsibility
- Human oversight
- Audit trails
- Feedback mechanisms

---

## Healthcare AI Ethics

### Patient Safety
- Thorough testing
- Clinical validation
- Continuous monitoring
- Clear failure modes

### Clinical Integration
- Augment, don't replace
- Appropriate trust
- Clinical judgment
- Shared decision-making

---

## Decision Framework

1. Who is affected?
2. What are the risks?
3. What are the benefits?
4. Are we being transparent?
5. Can we justify this decision?
