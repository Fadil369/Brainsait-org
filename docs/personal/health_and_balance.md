# Health & Balance

## Overview

Sustainable high performance requires attention to physical health, mental wellness, and life balance.

---

## Physical Health

### Exercise
- Strength training 3x/week
- Cardio 3x/week
- Daily movement
- Recovery days

### Nutrition
- Whole foods
- Adequate protein
- Hydration
- Mindful eating

### Sleep
- 7-8 hours
- Consistent schedule
- Sleep hygiene
- Wind-down routine

---

## Mental Wellness

### Stress Management
- Regular exercise
- Meditation/breathwork
- Nature time
- Social connection

### Mindfulness
- Daily meditation
- Mindful moments
- Body awareness
- Present focus

### Boundaries
- Work hours
- Device-free time
- Say no gracefully
- Protect recovery

---

## Life Balance

### Domains
- Work/career
- Relationships
- Health
- Personal growth
- Recreation
- Contribution

### Regular Assessment
- What's working?
- What needs attention?
- What to start/stop?

---

## Warning Signs

- Chronic fatigue
- Irritability
- Poor sleep
- Decreased motivation
- Social withdrawal

---

## Recovery Practices

- Regular vacations
- Weekly sabbath
- Daily micro-recovery
- Hobby time
