# Reflections

## Overview

Regular reflection is essential for growth, learning, and alignment with purpose.

---

## Reflection Practices

### Daily
- What went well?
- What could improve?
- What did I learn?
- What am I grateful for?

### Weekly
- Review accomplishments
- Assess priorities
- Plan next week
- Energy check

### Monthly
- Review goals
- Assess systems
- Celebrate progress
- Adjust direction

### Quarterly
- Big picture review
- Goal assessment
- Priority reset
- Future planning

### Annual
- Year in review
- Lessons learned
- Vision for next year
- Life audit

---

## Journaling

### Types
- Gratitude
- Morning pages
- Project logs
- Decision journals

### Questions
- What's on my mind?
- What am I avoiding?
- What would I do if I couldn't fail?
- What matters most right now?

---

## Life Review

### Questions
- What gives me energy?
- What drains me?
- What would I regret not doing?
- What legacy do I want?

### Areas to Review
- Career/work
- Relationships
- Health
- Finances
- Growth
- Contribution
