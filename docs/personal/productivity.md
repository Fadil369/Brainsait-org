# Productivity

## Overview

Systems and practices for maximizing productivity while maintaining balance and quality.

---

## Productivity Framework

### Capture
- Inbox zero approach
- Single capture point
- Quick processing

### Organize
- Projects and areas
- Next actions
- Waiting for
- Someday/maybe

### Execute
- Time blocking
- Deep work
- Batch similar tasks
- Minimize switching

### Review
- Daily review
- Weekly review
- Monthly review
- Quarterly planning

---

## Time Management

### Time Blocking
```
06:00-07:00  Morning routine
07:00-09:00  Deep work
09:00-10:00  Meetings
10:00-12:00  Deep work
12:00-13:00  Lunch + learning
13:00-15:00  Collaborative work
15:00-16:00  Admin/email
16:00-17:00  Planning/review
```

### Energy Management
- Match task to energy
- Protect peak hours
- Take breaks
- Manage transitions

---

## Focus Techniques

### Pomodoro
- 25 min work
- 5 min break
- After 4, long break

### Deep Work
- Distraction-free
- Single task
- Extended periods
- High cognitive demand
